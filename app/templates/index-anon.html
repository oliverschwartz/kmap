Why use Study Graph?

Study Graph is an online mind mapping tool you can use to take notes, review material, and collaborate with peers. Visualizing complex concepts in a simple way helps you structure your studying and retain information for longer. Our intuitive interface makes it easy to come away with the "bigger picture" in what you're learning.

Visualize

Unlike traditional, pure text methods for note taking and reviewing, mind maps use memory triggers like colors and arrows that that can enhance your brain's recall. Additionally, your graphs can help you connect new pieces of information to what you already know.

Access Anywhere

Study Graph is completely web-based, so you don't need to worry about downloads or updates. Unlike pen and paper mind maps, your graphs are ready to access at any time. Thanks to the Auto-Save feature, they'll always be up-to-date on any device you use.

Share

If you build a graph you'd like someone else to see, sharing is easy. You can send a link to friends to collaborate and pool knowledge, just like you would for any in-person study group. If you'd just like to share the graph as is, you can also download it as a static image.


Features

What sets Study Graph apart from other tools for note-taking and review?

Intuitive Visuals

Your graphs will highlight and display information for the nodes you click or hover on.

Simple Commands

The Command Line Interface (CLI) lets you build and edit your graphs on the fly.

Easy Search

Every graph automatically builds its own concept list for you to scroll and search through.
